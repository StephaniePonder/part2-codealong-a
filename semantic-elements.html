DC superheroes

DC superhero comics are the best.

Marvel superheroes or whatever

Marvel superhero comics feel more two dimensional. However, Marvel Comics is really killing it in the film and TV idustries, whereas DC's attempts to capitalize on TV and film are more mediocre.

Conclusion
